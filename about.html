Our Story

From passion to expertise

SLAMA Consulting was born from a deep passion for software development and technological innovation. Founded with the conviction that every business deserves quality digital solutions, our agency supports organizations of all sizes in their digital evolution.

Our journey began with mobile development, where we quickly gained recognized expertise in native Android. The creation of SportTracking, our flagship GPS sport tracking application, perfectly illustrates our ability to design complete products, from concept to production on the Play Store.

Today, we offer a comprehensive range of services covering mobile and web development, cloud computing, IT consulting, and digital transformation. Our approach combines technical rigor, creativity, and attentive listening to our clients' needs.
