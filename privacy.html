Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SLAMA Consulting (hereinafter referred to as "we," "our," or "the Company") collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal information of users of the SportTracking mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the Application"). By using the Application, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Data We Collect

In the course of using the Application, we may collect the following categories of data:

  • Location data: real-time GPS coordinates for tracking your sports activities, routes taken, distance, speed, and altitude.
  • Account data: email address, username, and profile photo when creating your account.
  • Activity data: history of your sports sessions, performance statistics, personal goals, and Ghost Race results.
  • Technical data: device type, operating system version, unique device identifier, error logs, and diagnostic data.
  • Usage data: interactions with the Application, features used, user preferences, and settings.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the features of the Application, including GPS tracking, performance history, and Ghost Race.
  • To personalize your user experience and provide relevant statistics and analysis of your sports activities.
  • To ensure the proper technical functioning of the Application, diagnose issues, and correct errors.
  • To send you notifications related to your activity, goals, and Application updates (with your consent).
  • To comply with our applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information may be shared in the following cases:

  • Service providers: we use trusted third-party providers (Firebase, Google Cloud) for hosting, analytics, and the proper functioning of the Application. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Social features: if you choose to share your sports activities through the Application's sharing features, certain data will be visible to the recipients you select.
  • Legal obligations: we may disclose your data if required by law or in response to a valid legal request from a competent authority.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
  • Secure authentication for access to user accounts.
  • Regular monitoring of our systems to detect vulnerabilities.
  • Limiting access to personal data to only those employees and contractors who need it.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We therefore cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide you with the Application's services. You may request the deletion of your account and data at any time. After your account is deleted, we may retain certain data in anonymized form for statistical purposes, or for the duration required by applicable legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: you may request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: you may request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to restriction of processing: you may request the restriction of the processing of your data in certain cases.
  • Right to data portability: you may request to receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: you may object to the processing of your data on legitimate grounds.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address provided below.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Application may use tracking technologies such as advertising identifiers and analytics tools to improve the user experience and understand how the Application is used. You can manage your tracking preferences in your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. In the event of a substantial change, we will notify you through the Application or by email. The last updated date at the top of this page indicates the current version in effect. We encourage you to review this Policy regularly.

9. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, to exercise your rights, or for any complaint relating to the protection of your personal data, you may contact us:

  • Email: slama.walid1985@gmail.com
  • Website: Contact page
  • Company: SLAMA Consulting, France

If you believe that the processing of your personal data constitutes a violation of your rights, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertes (CNIL) or any other competent supervisory authority.
